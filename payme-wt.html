Pay Me, Dude!

A basic online registry about those stingy people whom owe you some cash. Just type their names, how much and we'll remember it for you.

How it works? Your user or account will be your personal storage. Use a nickname or email. Select an option or action from the list, for starters: select Add / Update, type a name and press REGISTER then we'll save that record. Use the default option (Show my debts) when you want to view your records.
